Blackjack Rules & Guide

Objective

Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21. Your score is computed by summing the values of your cards.

Card Values

  • 2-10: Face value.
  • J, Q, K: 10 points.
  • Ace: 1 or 11 points (whichever benefits your hand most).

Game Actions

  • HIT: Request another card.
  • STAND: Keep your current hand and end your turn.
  • DOUBLE DOWN: Double your initial bet, receive exactly one more card, and stand.
  • SPLIT: If dealt a pair (two cards of equal value), you can split them into two separate hands by matching your original bet. Each hand is then played sequentially.
  • INSURANCE: If the dealer's upcard is an Ace, you can pay half your bet to insure against the dealer having Blackjack. Pays 2:1 if they do; loses otherwise.

Payouts

  • Standard Win: 1:1
  • Natural Blackjack (first two cards): 3:2
  • Insurance Win: 2:1
  • Tie (Push): Your bet is returned.
